Report on Food Delivery Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of customer orders, restaurant performance, and payment behavior. 

A total of 2,746 orders were placed, covering approximately 2.75K food items, indicating strong overall demand.

North Indian restaurants contribute the highest quantity of orders, followed by South Indian and Mughlai cuisines. 

Among food items, popular dishes like Biryani, Butter Chicken, and Chole show higher order quantities, highlighting customer preferences.

In terms of customers, Gold members place more orders compared to Regular members, and they show a higher delivery completion rate. 

UPI is the most preferred payment method, especially among Gold members, followed by Cash on Delivery (COD) and Card payments.

Overall, the dashboard indicates that premium members, popular cuisines,

and digital payment methods play a key role in driving order volume and successful deliveries.
